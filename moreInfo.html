About Wellness Buddy

We believe that mental and physical health go hand in hand, both can always use improvement and Wellness Buddy can help you simultaneously improve both. Wellness Buddy is designed to motivate and encorage people like you to live healthy and active lifestyles. Our website functions as a platform for you to find people with similar fitness goals and physical abilities, track your mental health, and log your workouts. Through our buddy-generating process, you will provide us with information about your goals, inspirations, and abilities. Such information will help us generate your ultimate Wellness Buddy! Our mood tracker allows you to log your emtions in an easy and accessible way. Loging your moods can create awarness and allow for you to see different trends in your moods that might be red flags.

Once you become a Wellness Buddy lover, you can purchase our premium version. Our premium version is $9.99/month and gives you unlimited access to Wellness Buddies and personalized training plans based on your goals and abilities. Additionally, the premium feature allows you to access maps that give you ideal and safe runnning routes around your area.
